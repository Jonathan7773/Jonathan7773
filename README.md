### About

*"How can we know?"* — I work at the intersection of statistics and computational neuroscience. I believe mathematical modeling and causal inference are the most rigorous tools we have for understanding the mind, and I'm building toward applying them to real-world problems.

Currently developing Active Inference modeling software at the Theoretical Neurobiology Unit (ETH Zürich), and serving as Neurobiology Chair at the Active Inference Institute — hosting weekly meetings with Prof. Karl Friston.
